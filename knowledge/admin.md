# Business Administration

## QuickBooks
- **QuickBooks Online (QBO)** — Primary accounting/invoicing platform
- Handles: invoicing, payroll, taxes, expense tracking
- QBO Payments for processing client payments
- **Payroll:** Processed through QuickBooks
- **Taxes:** Managed through QuickBooks; annual filing
- Business lead manages day-to-day billing (2-4 hours/month between renewals and invoice adjustments)

## Insurance
- **Business insurance policies** maintained annually
- Renewed on annual cycle
- Details and policy numbers in Dashlane/business records

## Business Licenses
- **Utah State business license** — annual renewal
- **Salt Lake City business license** — annual renewal
- Keep renewal dates tracked to avoid lapses

## Addresses

## Financial Accounts

### Banking
- **Chase** — Primary business checking/banking
- **American Express** — Business credit card

### Payment Processing
- **PayPal** — Some client payments and vendor payments
- **Stripe** — Payment processing for client sites with e-commerce/donations

## Contracts & RFP Sources
- RFPs (Requests for Proposal) come from various sources
- Proposals created in Proposify
- Typical flow: 30-min conversation + 30-60 min to send proposal
- Past proposals are reused and customized per client

## Essential Contacts
- Key vendor contacts, hosting support contacts, and partner contacts maintained in team records
- Emergency contacts should be accessible to multiple team members (not just one person)
